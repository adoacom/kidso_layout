DESCRIPTION

AP Hong Kong and the charitable AP Foundation and AP School were founded to meet the tremendous need for effective comprehensive services for families with children with autism in Hong Kong. Our Hong Kong office specializes in Contemporary Behavioral Therapy (CBT) that is based on the techniques of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) to help children with autism acquire new skills, curb unwanted behaviors, and achieve a better quality of life.
